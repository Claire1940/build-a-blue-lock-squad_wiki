<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Build a Blue Lock Squad Progression Guide: Core Training">

To successfully assemble a competitive team, players must master the core training loop. In Blue Lock: Project World Champion, newly acquired three-star characters cannot be immediately placed into your active lineup. They default to a locked G-rank. To unlock them for squad selection, you must run them through the specialized Training Mode to reach at least D+ rank.

During training, your primary objective is to manage a 24-turn countdown. Every action you take reduces this counter, boosting your player's base attributes such as Kick, Speed, and Physical strength. Balancing these stats is essential for creating specialized players who can fulfill specific tactical roles on the pitch.

| Player Rank | Usability Status | BP Potential | Recommended Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **G Rank** | Locked (Unusable) | Extremely Low | Needs immediate training |
| **F to D Rank** | Locked (Unusable) | Low | Complete training loop |
| **D+ to C Rank** | Unlocked (Usable) | Moderate | Budget squad fillers |
| **B to A Rank** | Unlocked (Usable) | High | Core squad starters |
| **S to SS+ Rank** | Unlocked (Usable) | Extremely High | Competitive meta leaders |

The training environment requires careful planning. If you complete the process successfully, your player's final rank will shoot up, dramatically increasing your total team Battle Power (BP). This is the absolute foundation of squad progression.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina and Session Warning">
Do not exit or abandon a training session halfway. Leaving a training run before the turn counter reaches zero will reset your accumulated stats, wasting your valuable stamina and leaving your character locked at G-rank.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Training Walkthrough">

To consistently achieve high ranks like A or S, you must follow a structured training routine. Randomly clicking training tiles will result in sub-optimal stat distribution and low final ranks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Character and Support Cards">
    Choose a high-potential three-star character and pair them with compatible support cards. Support cards provide passive multipliers that significantly boost the stat gains from your training actions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the 24-Turn Countdown">
    Carefully select training tiles like muscle training, tactical drills, or sprinting. Your goal is to drop the counter from 24 to 0 while keeping your player's stamina high enough to avoid training failures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Win Scheduled Training Matches">
    Participate in scheduled matches during the training run. Winning these matches provides massive stat boosts. If your temporary BP is significantly higher than the opponent's, you can safely skip the match to save time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Final Evaluation">
    Once the counter hits zero, complete the final match of the training cycle. The game will evaluate your performance and assign a permanent rank, unlocking the character for your main squad.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Different player positions require different stat profiles. Strikers need high Kick and Speed, while midfielders require excellent Technique and Stamina. Use the table below to target the right stats during your training runs.

| Archetype | Primary Stat Focus | Key Training Tiles | Target Skill Types |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Striker** | Kick, Speed | Muscle Training, Sprinting | Shoot Power, Finishing |
| **Midfielder** | Technique, Stamina | Tactical Drills, Passing | Playmaking, Ball Control |
| **Defender** | Physical, Defense | Strength Training, Tackle | Interception, Marking |

<Callout type="tip" title="Optimize Your Support Cards">
Always match the color and stat bonuses of your support cards with the training character's natural strengths. A striker benefits significantly more from Kick-boosting support cards than Defense-boosting ones.
</Callout>
